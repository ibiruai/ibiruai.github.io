EVILEYE # SUPERHERO # SENTRY was a Counter-Strike 1.6 server with SuperHero Mod and Sentry Guns. Launched 2 February 2019, it worked until 2 May 2021. Its distinguishing features were Russian translation, display of cooldowns, the mod menu to replace chat commands, the auto-generated MOTD with powers descriptions, new powers ported from other plugins, bots being able to use active powers.
